Hi, I'm shaggsteru. Some random Romanian guy on the internet who likes playing around with computers.

You are more than welcome to look around, read and interact with my website. I will try to grow this website along with my skills in HTML and CSS, so expect changes over time.

Started this project back in 2024, when I discovered a video made by Eric Murphy talking about the importance of the personal website. It inspired me to create my own personal space on the internet where I could talk about my personal interests and share my hobbies with other people. Shortly after that, I discovered this movement of people creating their own websites to have a place where they could share their creativity and passions, one of them is neocities. I initially planned on using neocities for this, but I already bought this domain, so I feel obligated to use it.

I will probably share mainly pictures of my cats, gym progress along with food recipes and supplements I use (I'm not a professional, so I advise you to check in multiple trusted sources), and reviews about the games, movies, and anime I watch.

Favorite quote: "Life was like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're gonna get."
